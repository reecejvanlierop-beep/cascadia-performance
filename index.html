Our Approach

This is not traditional chiropractic care.

Most care focuses on short-term relief. We focus on why the problem exists in the first place — and how to fix it for good.

Our approach is built around identifying root causes, restoring proper movement, and building a body that can handle real life. We combine hands-on treatment, movement correction, and strength development to create results that last.

Our goal isn't to keep you coming back forever. It's to give you the tools and the understanding to stay healthy long after you leave the clinic.

The People Behind Your Care

Meet Our Team

Dr. Robert Friendt, DC

Chiropractor

Born and raised in Northern Minnesota, Dr. Friendt was a Division I pole vaulter and decathlete at the University of Minnesota before earning his Doctorate from Texas Chiropractic College.

He brings over 10 years of experience from the Houston Medical Center, where he worked alongside University of Texas Orthopedics, Rice University, and Houston Baptist University athletics. Now based in Spokane with his wife and daughter, he is passionate about the outdoors and helping patients move and live better.

Reece Van Lierop

Rehab Specialist

A Spokane native and Ferris High School graduate, Reece earned his degree in Applied Kinesiology from Corban University. He joined the Cascadia Performance team in 2024 with a genuine passion for patient-centered care.

Reece is dedicated to listening closely to each patient's experience and supporting them throughout their healing journey — helping bridge the gap between treatment and long-term functional performance.
